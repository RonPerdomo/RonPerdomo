## About

I spent 5 years as an **FDNY firefighter** and 3+ years at the **NYC Law Department** before moving into cybersecurity. Those environments didn't teach me theory — they taught me what happens when protocols break down, when documentation is wrong, and when accountability is optional. (Spoiler: people get hurt.)

That operational background is the foundation I bring to GRC, IAM, and SOC work:

- **FDNY** → real-time risk assessment, incident response, compliance inspections, post-incident documentation
- **NYC Law Department** → data classification, records management, QA control testing, strict confidentiality protocols
- **Charter/Spectrum** → 1,000+ tickets/week, SLA compliance, control failure documentation, process improvement

Currently building hands-on security skills under the mentorship of a **CIO-level cybersecurity leader with 20+ years in GRC, IAM, and Enterprise Controls**.
